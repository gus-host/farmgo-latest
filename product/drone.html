PRODUCT

Drone services

Need a platform to monitor and manage a 104 acres (will soon be more) oil palm plantation. It’s impossible for human to walk more than 10 acres in a day making management of the farm difficult for human.

Feature request:

Under the oil palm tree canopy
1. Identify fruit that are ripe, unripe and possible overripe
2. Identity and label each tree for yield monitoring and mapping
3. Estimate/classify the size of each fruit (big, small, medium)
4. Geo-reference image so we can know the location of each fruit.

On top of the tree canopy

1. Count the number of trees
2. Identify bush burning, fire, smoke and alert the admin.
